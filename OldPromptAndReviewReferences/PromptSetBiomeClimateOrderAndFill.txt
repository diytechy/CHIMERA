The final "to" children of "biome-distribution\stages\climate\temperature.yml" follows an order of coldest to hotest.  The fields of "biome-distribution\stages\climate\precipitation.yml" should follow a similar order, where the high level order goes from hotest to coldest, subdivided from most dry to most wet.  Update the order of the "biome-distribution\stages\climate\precipitation.yml" so that this convention is followed.  Note the order of biome type should also be respected (land / coast / ocean / mesa / crater lake / extinct-volcano / island /vast-forest).

      boreal-snowy:

        - boreal-snowy: *mid
        - boreal-snowy: *mildlyWet
        - boreal-snowy: *veryWet
