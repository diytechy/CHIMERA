## Specific Implementation Examples

### Example 1: Adding Coastal Biomes to fill_coasts.yml

```yaml
# For cold coasts
  - type: REPLACE
    from: coast_small_cold
    sampler:
      type: CELLULAR
      jitter: ${customization.yml:biomeSpread.cellJitter}
      return: CellValue
      frequency: 1 / ${customization.yml:biomeSpread.cellDistance}
      salt: 8726345
    to:
      SHALE_BEACH: 1
      FROZEN_BEACH: 1
      BEACH: 2
      SNOWDRIFT_COASTS: 1

# For warm coasts
  - type: REPLACE
    from: coast_small_warm
    sampler:
      type: CELLULAR
      jitter: ${customization.yml:biomeSpread.cellJitter}
      return: CellValue
      frequency: 1 / ${customization.yml:biomeSpread.cellDistance}
      salt: 8726345
    to:
      PALM_BEACH: 4
      BEACH: 2
      SHRUB_BEACH: 1
      MUDDY_COASTS: 1
```
